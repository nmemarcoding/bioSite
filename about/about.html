Meet Damoon Sala

Damoon Sala, born and raised in the small town of Kerman, Iran, is a passionate and dedicated professional guitarist and instructor. His journey into the world of music was anything but easy. Growing up in a place with limited access to quality music education, Damoon’s desire to learn guitar pushed him to extraordinary lengths. Every week, he would embark on a grueling 6-hour bus ride to Shiraz – a larger city with better opportunities – just to attend guitar lessons. This relentless dedication and commitment to his craft laid the foundation for his successful career.

Over the years, Damoon has evolved into one of Iran’s most recognized pop guitarists. His unique playing style, influenced by both Western and Persian music, has earned him a loyal following. Beyond performing on stage with renowned Iranian pop artists, he has also found great joy in teaching. As a dedicated instructor, Damoon takes pride in mentoring aspiring musicians, helping them navigate the complexities of technique, theory, and performance.

Damoon’s musical journey is a testament to perseverance and passion. He has performed in numerous concerts across Iran, showcasing his versatile skills and love for music. Looking ahead, Damoon aspires to expand his reach by releasing instructional content online and collaborating with international artists to bring Iranian music to a global audience.

When he’s not playing the guitar, Damoon enjoys exploring nature and drawing inspiration from his surroundings. His story is one of determination, talent, and an unshakable love for music.
